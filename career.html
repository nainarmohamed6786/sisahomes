Our Vacancies

SISA HOMES is developing rapidly and we need cool & creative architects to work together and to create something unique, cool, thoughtful and functional designs.

To Do

  • Design and develop cool design forms and ideas.
  • Create architectural designs based on Client inputs.
  • Prepare and execute client contracts pursuant to specific consultations.
  • Co-Ordinating with the team and developing Architectural designs & details.
  • Preparing Working/detail drawings like Electrical, Plumbing & Carpentry drawings.
  • Good presentation skills.
  • Communicate with contractors and construction workers to implement designs.

If

  • Experienced and skilled in the design and development of Residential or Commercial design projects (Min 1 - 2 yrs. of experience).
  • Proficient in: AutoCAD, SketchUp, Enscape, Lumion, Photoshop & Microsoft
  • Knowledge of Building products & Materials with construction details.
  • Strong imagination and the ability to think and create in three dimensions
  • Visual awareness and an eye for detail
