About Us

With SISA Construction, living in your dream home is closer than you think!

Headquartered in Bangalore, SISA HOMES is an established construction company that provides turnkey building, designing and constructional services for residential, commercial and industrial sectors in Bangalore, Chennai and Hubli-Dharwad. SISA HOME’S in-house design and engineering team incorporate advanced technology and innovative methodology to execute comprehensive construction projects in and around Bangalore.

With efficient approaches to construction, SISA HOMES put forth its valuable expertise as one of the best civil construction companies in Bangalore. Our main area of construction includes, Residential Construction and Commercial & Institutional Construction. With an overall experience of 10 years in the construction industry, SISA HOMES promises every client our top notch quality services, best-in-class materials, hassle free user experience from initial designs to final handover, zero delayed project deliveries, absolute transparency in billings, complete reliability and extended warranty.
